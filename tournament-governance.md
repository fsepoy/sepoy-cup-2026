# Sepoy Cup — Tournament Governance

Rules and procedures for the Sepoy Cup. First edition: 2026 · FC24 · Family Cup.

## Tournament Format

- **6 nations** drawn into **2 groups of 3**
- **Group Stage:** Round-robin within each group (3 matches per group, 6 total)
- **Knockout Stage:** Top 2 from each group advance to semi-finals
- **Semi-Final 1:** 1st Group A vs 2nd Group B
- **Semi-Final 2:** 1st Group B vs 2nd Group A
- **3rd Place Match:** SF1 loser vs SF2 loser
- **Final:** SF1 winner vs SF2 winner

## Points System (Group Stage)

| Result | Points |
|---|---|
| Win | 3 |
| Draw | 1 |
| Loss | 0 |

Points are **only awarded in the group stage**. Knockout matches always produce a winner.

## Group Ranking Order

1. Points
2. Goal Difference (GD)
3. Goals Scored (GF)
4. Tie-Break Match (if teams are equal after Points and GD)

## Tie-Breaker Rules

### Two-Team Tie

If two teams finish level after the group stage:

1. **Tie-Break Match** — a full match played between the tied teams
2. If still level after normal time → **Tie-Break Mini Tournament** format:

| Match | Format |
|---|---|
| TB1 | Full Match |
| TB2 | Full Match |
| TB3 (if needed) | Half Match |

**Ranking in Mini Tournament:**
1. Points
2. Goal Difference (GD)
3. Goals Scored
4. **Sudden-Death Penalty Shootout**

### Three-Team Tie

If all three teams in a group are tied on the same record:

1. Points within the three-way mini-league
2. Goal Difference (GD)
3. Goals Scored
4. Sudden-Death Penalty Shootout

## Knockout Match Rules

- **Normal time result** stands after full time (no draws — use extra time/penalties if needed)
- **Extra Time:** Played if tied after normal time
- **Penalty Shootout:** If still level after extra time
- **Winner advances**, loser is eliminated (or takes 3rd place berth)

## Spirit of the Sepoy Cup

> This is more than a tournament — "This is legacy in the making."
